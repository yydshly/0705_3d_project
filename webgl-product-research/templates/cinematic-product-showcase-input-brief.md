## 8. Quality Gate

The result is not acceptable if:

- It only rotates the camera.
- Parts do not have clear purpose.
- Visual effects are just decoration and do not explain a feature.
- Text explains everything while 3D does not carry the story.
- The product proportions look wrong.
- The timeline feels like unrelated shots instead of one product story.
